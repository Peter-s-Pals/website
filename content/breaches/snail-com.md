In March 2015, the gaming website <a href="https://www.technadu.com/emuparadise-1-1-million-user-data-breach/70025/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Snail suffered a data breach</a> that impacted 1.4 million subscribers. The impacted data included usernames, IP and email addresses and passwords stored as unsalted MD5 hashes.
