In approximately March 2017, the file sharing website <a href="http://bolt.cd/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Bolt</a> suffered a data breach resulting in the exposure of 995k unique user records. The data was sourced from their vBulletin forum and contained email and IP addresses, usernames and salted MD5 password hashes.
